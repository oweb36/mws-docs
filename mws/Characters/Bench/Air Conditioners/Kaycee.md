# Kaycee

Kaycee is a [character](Characters.md) as a backgrounder on the [Megarchive Warehouse Show](Megarchive%20Warehouse%20Show.md).
## Identity

Kaycee is a Daikin FTKC15NBM4 [air conditioner](../../Species/Air%20Conditioners.md) with an RKC15NVM4 outdoor unit. She's a white smile inverter from Indonesia, not a star inverter. 

Kaycee was born in June 7, 2015. She's a female (she/her) who's straight cus yeah.
