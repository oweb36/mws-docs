## Personality
> TLDR: Cowgirl. Quite strict, rugged, stoic, individual, hard-working, workaholic. She's surprisingly also plucky and perky.
> TLDR2: Also that smoker and tobacco chewer, she does look pretty edgy doing so. Smoking does make her look like a bitch, which she kinda is, but isn't also.

She's a cowgirl. She is quite strict with her work, and stoic. She doesn't really show a lot of emotions, and in fact is quite strict and pretty obsessive with a work. A pretty workaholic if you ask me. A stoic one, not a lot of emotions to show (but she's also perky). And she's also very individualistic, opting to work alone instead of teaming.

Surprisingly, she's also quite perky and plucky. She's very determined and spirited. And Terra is also quite cheerful and lively if you can poke the right buttons. Despite her being stoic, Terra can show emotions and livelihood. When she does so, it's very heartwarming. When she's perky and plucky, she's very nice. Willing to work together, dropping the individualism.

She's also a cigarette smoker and a tobacco chewer. She looks very edgy and cynical when doing so. It makes her look like a bitch, which she kinda is (individualism and workaholism), but is also not rlly either (perky and plucky).
